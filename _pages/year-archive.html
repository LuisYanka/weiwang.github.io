Fall 2024

In this seminar, we mainly discuss the book "Introduction to Geometric Measure Theory" by L. Simon (abbreviated as [Sim14] in the following table). We also refer to the book "Measure Theory and Fine Properties of Functions" for additional references. The reading seminar is held mostly every Thursday and Sunday afternoon from 14:00 to 16:00 at the Jiayibing or Zhihua buildings of Peking University. Occasionally, due to the special schedules of the attendees, the time may change, and the seminar may be held online.

Attendees (Alphabetical)

  • Haotong Fu (Ph.D. candidate, PKU)
  • Huaijie Wang (Postdoctoral fellow, PKU)
  • Wei Wang (Ph.D. candidate, PKU)

Contents of the Seminar

  • Ch. 4 of [Sim14]: The Allard Regularity Theorem. 2025/1/12, 14:00-16:30. Room 313, Zhihua Building. Lecturer: Haotong Fu.
  • Ch. 4 of [Sim14]: Theory of Rectifiable n-Varifolds. 2025/1/6, 14:00-16:00. Room 217, Zhihua Building. Lecturer: Huaijie Wang.
  • Ch. 3 of [Sim14]: Reduced Boundaries of Sets with Finite Perimeters. 2024/12/28, 14:00-16:00. Room 313, Zhihua Building. Lecturer: Wei Wang.
  • Ch. 3 of [Sim14]: Preliminary Results of BV Functions. 2024/12/27, 15:00-17:00. Room 313, Zhihua Building. Lecturer: Wei Wang.
  • Ch. 3 of [Sim14]: Rectifiable Sets. 2024/12/22, 13:00-15:00. Room 313, Zhihua Building. Lecturer: Wei Wang.
  • Ch. 1 & 2 of [Sim14]: Some Results for Measure Theory and Submanifolds. 2024/12/15, 14:00-16:00. Underground discussion room, Jiayibing Building. Lecturer: Haotong Fu.

Spring 2023

In this seminar, we primarily study recent breakthroughs in harmonic maps surrounding the following two papers:
  • "Quantitative Stratification and the Regularity of Harmonic Maps and Minimal Currents" by Jeff Cheeger and Aaron Naber (abbreviated as [CN13])
  • "Rectifiable-Reifenberg and the Regularity of Stationary and Minimizing Harmonic Maps" by Aaron Naber and Daniele Valtorta (abbreviated as [NV17])
The reading seminar is held mostly every Friday evening from 18:40 to 20:40 in Science Building No. 2 of Peking University.

Attendees (Alphabetical)

  • Yuxuan Chen (Ph.D. student, PKU)
  • Haotong Fu (Undergraduate student, PKU)
  • Yuning Liu (Professor, NYU Shanghai)
  • Wei Wang (Ph.D. student, PKU)
  • Zhifei Zhang (Professor, PKU)

Contents of the Seminar

  • Ch. 7-11 of [NV17]: Proof of Main Results. 2023/4/21, 18:40-20:40. Room 2517, Science Building No. 2. Lecturer: Wei Wang.
  • Ch. 3-6 of [NV17]: Rectifiable Reifenberg Results II. 2023/4/7, 18:30-20:30. Room 2517, Science Building No. 2. Lecturer: Wei Wang.
  • Learning report about [CN13] and [NV17]. 2025/4/2. Room 2517, Science Building No. 2. Lecturers: Haotong Fu and Wei Wang.
  • Learning Report on [CN13] and [NV17]. 2024/4/1, 14:00-17:00. Room 1560, Science Building No. 1. Lecturers: Yuxuan Chen and Wei Wang.
  • Ch. 3-6 of [NV17]: Rectifiable Reifenberg Results I. 2023/3/23, 18:30-20:30. Room 2517, Science Building No. 2. Lecturer: Yuxuan Chen.
  • Classical Reifenberg Theorem. 2023/3/17, 18:40-20:30. Room 2517, Science Building No. 2. Lecturer: Yuxuan Chen.
  • The Quantitative Stratification. 2023/2/24, 18:40-20:30. Room 2517, Science Building No. 2. Lecturer: Yuxuan Chen.
